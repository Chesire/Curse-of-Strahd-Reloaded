## Appearance

Thorn appears as a young boy of around eight to ten years old. He can be seen depicted in the Durst family portrait that hangs above the fireplace in Death House's main hall, holding a smiling doll wearing a yellow lace dress. As a ghostly spirit, he retains his youthful appearance and is rarely seen without clinging closely to his older sister Rose.

## Character Information

### Persona

**To the world:** Thorn is a frightened young boy who clings to his sister. He appears shy and withdrawn, seeking comfort in Rose's presence.

**To those he trusts:** Thorn is a quietly observant and insightful child. Beneath his anxiety, he possesses insight and understanding beyond his years.

### Morale in Combat

### Relationships

- **Rosavalda "Rose" Durst:** His older sister. Thorn is entirely dependent on Rose for emotional support and safety. He rarely strays far from her side.
- **Walter Durst:** His half-sibling. Thorn's relationship with Walter is unknown, though he is wary of him.
- **Elisabeth and Gustav Durst:** His parents. Thorn remembers them with a mix of love and the trauma of Death House.
- **Klara:** His nursemaid (known to Thorn as "Miss Klara"). Rose has memories of the nursemaid recorded in her diary.
