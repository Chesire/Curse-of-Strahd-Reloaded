# Elisabeth Durst

> [!info]+ **Quick Reference**
> - **Origin:** Death House, Barovia
> - **Status:** Deceased; Ghost/Spirit
> - **Relationships:** Mother of [[Rosavalda Durst|Rose]], [[Thornboldt Durst|Thorn]], and [[Walter Durst]]; Wife of [[Gustav Durst]]
> - **Primary Location:** Death House

## Appearance

Elisabeth is depicted in the family portrait hanging above the Main Hall fireplace alongside [[Gustav Durst|Gustav]] and their children. As a spirit, she manifests in various forms throughout Death House:

- In the Master Suite, a silhouette behind stained-glass doors, lit by dim amber glow
- In the Conservatory, a ghostly apparition watching her husband dance with another woman
- As a grotesquely distorted spirit during the escape from Death House—hair wild and disheveled, skin deathly pale, lips peeled back to reveal sharp teeth, with an amber shard glowing on a cord around her spectral neck

## Roleplaying Information

### Resonance

Elisabeth should inspire:
- **Horror** at her presence and manifestations
- **Dread** at the supernatural phenomena she controls

### Emotions

Elisabeth most often feels: **furious**, **malevolent**, **commanding**

### Motivations

Elisabeth commands Death House and its trapped spirits. She uses the amber shard to control [[Walter Durst|Walter]].

## Character Information

### Persona

Elisabeth is the dominating supernatural force within Death House. Her rage and will bind the spirits trapped within its walls.

### Relationships

- **[[Gustav Durst]]:** Her husband. Elisabeth's apparition shows cold fury when observing him with [[Klara|the nursemaid]]
- **[[Rosavalda Durst|Rose]] and [[Thornboldt Durst|Thorn]]:** Her children
- **[[Walter Durst]]:** Her other child, bound through dark magic and the amber shard
- **[[Klara]]:** The nursemaid. Elisabeth tortured her and bound her spirit to Death House

## Background & History

Elisabeth is a deceased spirit haunting Death House. A rolled parchment in the Master Suite, addressed to "Drasha," references Elisabeth's instructions regarding the amber shard and its use to control [[Walter Durst|Walter]].

## Key Items

**The Amber Shard:** Found in the Master Suite's jewelry box, this magical artifact is described in the accompanying note as capable of weakening and soothing the Beast. When presented and the Beast's name is spoken with conviction, it will obey the holder's commands, at least temporarily.
